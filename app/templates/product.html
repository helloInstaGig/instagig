INSTAGIG FEATURES

One App. Many Opportunities.

Sign Up

  • Freelancers & Clients: sign up with your contact & bank information (ages 18+)
  • Freelancers & Clients: customize your profiles to your needs
  • Freelancers: go through a verification process done by our team

Posting

  • Clients: make a post of the job you want completed
  • Freelancers: click "I'm Interested" button if you're interested in a post
  • Clients: view profiles of freelancers who expressed interest and choose who you want to hire
  • Clients: see sample work from freelancers to help you decide
  • Freelancers: advertise your skills and projects to stand out

Payment

  • Clients & Freelancers: negotiate pricing through our messaging system
  • Freelancers: charged a commission fee depending on the price of the service
  • We hold onto your funds until the job is complete and both parties are satisfied
  • Credit cards/ Debit cards/ Mobile payment methods (Apple Pay, Paypal)

Pricing

For every job completed, we charge freelancers a commission fee based on the price of the service.

Service Cost: <$50

15% commission

For jobs costing less than $50, we earn 15% commission. Example jobs include walking the dog, washing a car, mowing a lawn, shoveling the snow, and so on.

Service Cost: $100-$300

20% commission

For jobs costing between $100 and $300, we earn a 20% commission. Jobs may include drawing artwork, creating a simple website or product.

Service Cost: >$300

25% commission

For jobs costing greater than $300, we earn a 25% commission. Jobs may include creating a complete professional website, creating a physical product like furniture, and others.
