WELCOME TO INSTAGIG

Our Mission

To connect the best freelancers to complete the jobs that matter to clients while giving freelancers greater flexibility and independence.

InstaGig: Why Was it Created?

The nature of the work environment continues to change, many people struggle to gain experience in their field or lack flexibilty in their 9-to-5 jobs. On the other hand, many clients have trouble accessing trustworthy and dedicated freelancers.

InstaGig was created to tackle these problems. Our extensive background checks on freelancers ensure that clients are matched with someone they can trust to get their job done. InstaGig also provides the independence and flexibility that people are seeking. Freelancers can showcase their skills and experience, and offer personalized services, ranging from IT development, content writing, accounting to babysitting or walking a dog all in one location.
